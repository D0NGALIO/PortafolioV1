Project 1 – Splend App

Splend is an educational and interactive mobile app designed to support children with Type 1 diabetes by transforming daily health management into an engaging and accessible experience. Through friendly visual design, gamified tasks, and intuitive monitoring tools, the app helps young users understand glucose levels, build healthy habits, and develop autonomy in their treatment. Splend combines user-centered research, playful characters, and clear visual indicators to create a supportive environment where learning about health becomes both empowering and enjoyable.

Project 2 – Karagabí

Karagabí is an immersive sensory installation inspired by the Embera Katío origin myth of water, blending sound design, shadow play, and atmospheric lighting to create an emotional and culturally rich experience. The project guides participants through an evocative narrative using layered natural soundscapes, symbolic silhouettes, and spatial storytelling that highlight the spiritual connection between ancestral traditions and the environment. Karagabí demonstrates how multimedia design can preserve and reinterpret indigenous cultural knowledge through expressive and immersive digital experiences.
